Help my people stay alive and keep resisting

I’m a designer from Ukraine. Too many of my relatives, friends and colleagues are serving right now — some of them have already been killed. This site is both my studio and a way to send targeted help to them.

  • Any amount matters — even 1 $ closes a small but real need on the front line.
  • Donations go to verified units and people I know personally for gear, drones, communication and medical supplies.
  • You have my real contacts here. I will answer everyone and report on how funds are spent.

Yes, you see news about corruption at the top in Ukraine. That problem exists, but it does not cancel the sacrifice of people in trenches and hospitals. They keep fighting every day and rely on direct support from those who refuse to look away.

Here you’re not just sending money into a void. You support my work as a designer and, at the same time, help my brothers and sisters in uniform have a better chance to come back home.

DONATE

Send what feels possible — 1 $, 5 $, 20 $ or more. I’ll regularly publish transparent reports on this site so you can see how your help turns into real action.

I am open to dialogue if you need deeper reporting about how your support is used or want to discuss specific details. Just please remember that, in the reality of the war against Ukraine, any shared details, screenshots or quotes from our communication can also be misused by hostile actors or people acting in bad faith. If you are unsure, please email me directly and we will choose a safe way to talk.
